Patna

Population: 58,38,465
Primary Language: Hindi

Places of Interest

Patna Planetarium
Located in Patna's Indira Gandhi Science complex, it is one of the largest planetariums in Asia. Patna Planetarium, or Taramandal, is modeled after the planet Saturn.

Gandhi Smarak Sangrahalaya
Also known as the Gandhi Memorial Institution, this museum showcases the life and principles of Mahatma Gandhi. 

Food
Malpua is a crisp and fluffy sweetened breakfast dish famous in Patna. Generally, this meal is served with tea as a meal, snack, or dessert depending on the time of day.
