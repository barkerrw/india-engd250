Places of Interest

The Charminar
Constructed in 1591, many people believe the Charminar was built to honor the elimination of a plague in the city. It was commissioned by the fifth Sultan of the Qutb Shahi dynasty as a grand mosque.

Golconda
This ruined city was originally built in the 11th century out of mud walls. After the discovery of diamond mines in the area, a fortress was built where the original city stood.

Food
A trip to Hyderabad would be incomplete without trying Hyderabdai Biryani. This biryani features basmati rice, meat, and grounded spices which offer a sharp, spicy aroma.
