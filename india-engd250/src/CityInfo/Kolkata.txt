Places of Interest

Victoria Memorial Hall
During the British's rule over India, this monument was built to honor the late Queen Victoria, Empress of India. It is the largest monument dedicated to a monarch anywhere in the world and is now a museum.

Dakshineswar Kali Temple
Built in 1855 by Rani Rashmoni, this temple features brilliant architecture and deep meaning for people of the Hindi religion. The presiding deity of the temple is Bhavatarini, a form of Kali.

Food
Machher Jhol is one of Kolkata's most famous foods. This traditional spicy fish curry is generally served as a stew or gravy over rice.
