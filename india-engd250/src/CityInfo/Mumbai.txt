Mumbai

Population: 21,297,000
Primary Language: Marathi

Places of Interest

Colaba Causeway
Also known as Shahid Bhagat Singh Road, it is a busy street famous for its wide variety of vendors and pubs.

Bandra-Worli Sea Link
Bandra is officially known as Rajiv Ghanid Sea Link. This landmark is an eight-lane cable bridge that connects Bandra and Worli. Construction began in 2000 and ended nine years later in June.

Food
One of the most common dishes in Mumbai is vada pav. Vada pav, or wada pao, consists of a deep-fried potato dumbling, or vada, in between two buns called pav. Generally, it is paired with green chutney.
